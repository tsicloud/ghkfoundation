Thank You for Donating

Your generosity helps provide instruments, equipment, and scholarships in Grant’s memory. We appreciate your support for the GHK Music Foundation.

We accept donations via Credit Card or ACH Bank Transfer. Our preferred method is ACH, as more of your donation goes directly to the foundation.

We use Stripe’s secure payment processing. Credit card donations incur a fee of 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction, while ACH transfers have a minimal flat fee of $1.00 per transaction. Your choice of payment helps us maximize the impact of your generosity.
